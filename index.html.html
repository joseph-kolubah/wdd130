Monrovia

Monrovia is the capital of Liberia. It is located on the West Coast of Africa, bordered by Guinea, Sierra Leone, Ivory Coast and the Atlantic Ocean. The city of Monrovia is very beautiful and welcoming to tourists. The city is home to many cultural artifacts, ancient places, and beautiful mountain, creeks and river to explore nature. Visitors enjoyed their experienced with the inhabitants and the good vibes around the nation.
